PARAGLIDING (MONTALBAN, RIZAL)

Paragliding in Montalban, Rizal offers an exhilarating adventure with breathtaking views of the Sierra Madre mountain range and the picturesque valleys below. Located just a short drive from Metro Manila, Montalban is becoming a popular spot for paragliding enthusiasts due to its ideal weather conditions, thermal winds, and panoramic landscapes. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced pilot, the area provides a thrilling experience, with safe launch sites and stunning vistas of lush forests, hills, and rivers. It’s an exciting way to explore the natural beauty of Rizal from a unique perspective.

CAZA PEREGRINE CAFE

Caza Peregrine Cafe is a cozy and charming café located in the scenic town of Montalban, Rizal. Known for its relaxing ambiance and beautiful views of the surrounding mountains, the café offers a peaceful retreat for visitors. The menu features a variety of coffee blends, pastries, and light meals, making it a perfect spot to unwind after a day of outdoor activities like paragliding or hiking. The café's warm, rustic interior and welcoming atmosphere make it an ideal place to enjoy a leisurely meal or catch up with friends.

MOUNTAIN CLIMBING

Mountain climbing at Nagpatong Rock in Tanay, Rizal is an exciting and challenging adventure, known for its unique rock formations and stunning views. This relatively difficult trek involves navigating through steep trails, rocky terrain, and a final climb up to the iconic Nagpatong Rock, which consists of towering limestone formations. Once at the summit, climbers are rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the Sierra Madre mountain range, the nearby hills, and lush valleys. The trek requires some technical skills and is ideal for experienced climbers looking for a more rugged and rewarding experience in Tanay’s natural beauty.

WAKEBOARDING

Wakeboarding in CamSur (Camarines Sur), Philippines, offers an exhilarating and unique water sport experience in one of the country’s most beautiful destinations. Located in the heart of the Bicol region, the CamSur Watersports Complex (CWC) is known as the premier wakeboarding park in the Philippines. It attracts both seasoned pros and beginners looking to enjoy the thrill of gliding across calm, pristine waters surrounded by lush, tropical scenery. The CWC features world-class facilities, including a cable system that allows riders to wakeboard without the need for a boat, making it an eco-friendly and cost-effective way to enjoy the sport.

SKYDIVE

Skydiving in Cebu, Philippines, is the ultimate thrill for adrenaline junkies looking to experience one of the most breathtaking views in the world. Known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning landscapes, Cebu provides a jaw-dropping backdrop for an unforgettable skydive adventure. The most popular location for skydiving is in the town of Bantayan, where you can freefall from thousands of feet in the air and take in panoramic views of Cebu’s coastline, islands, and turquoise waters.
